Rafting Site Plan

Syla Marie Cumuyog

Overview

Purpose

The purpose of establishing an online presence for my rafting business is multifaceted. Firstly, it's about accessibility and convenience for potential customers. By having a website and social media profiles, we make it easier for people to find information about our services, check availability, and even book trips online. Secondly, it's about visibility and marketing. An online presence allows us to reach a broader audience, including those who may not have heard of us through traditional means. We can showcase our unique offerings, share customer testimonials, and engage with our audience through content and interactions. Ultimately, the goal is to increase brand awareness, attract more customers, and grow the business.

Audience

Our water rafting website is designed to cater primarily to adventure enthusiasts, thrill-seekers, and outdoor enthusiasts who are seeking exhilarating experiences in nature. Our target audience includes individuals aged 18-45, who are physically active, enjoy outdoor activities, and are looking for unique and exciting adventures to embark on.

Questions: 1. Who will use the site and how will they use it? Answer: Our target audience consists of adventure seekers who are likely to visit the site to gather information about our rafting trips, explore available packages, check trip dates and availability, and ultimately make bookings for their desired adventure experience. They may also use the site to learn about safety measures, equipment requirements, and the overall experience we provide. 2. What are the key interests and preferences of our target audience? Answer: Our target audience is passionate about outdoor activities and adventure sports. They seek adrenaline-pumping experiences and value activities that allow them to connect with nature. They are likely to be interested in the level of difficulty of our rafting trips, the scenic beauty of the locations we operate in, and the overall thrill factor of the experience. Additionally, they may prioritize safety standards, knowledgeable guides, and well-maintained equipment. 3. How can the website meet the needs and preferences of our target audience effectively? Answer: To effectively meet the needs and preferences of our target audience, the website should feature clear and concise information about our rafting trips, including trip durations, difficulty levels, available amenities, and pricing. It should also showcase stunning visuals of the rafting locations and highlight customer testimonials to build trust and credibility. Additionally, the website should offer easy navigation, mobile responsiveness, and seamless booking functionality to ensure a smooth user experience for our adventurous clientele.
